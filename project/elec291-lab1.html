ELEC 291 Lab 1 - LCD Demo

This lab's objective is to display the name and student number on an LCD display with an 8051 microcontroller. It's the first lab of the design course to help students learn 8051 assembly programming. As a fun extra, there's also a song and some cool visual effects added.
